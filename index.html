SA Journos' Top Tweets

We track South African journalists' tweets and rank them by retweets and favourites. This list shows the top ten SA journos' tweets by retweets and favourites for original tweets in the past 24 hours.

The list of journos is based on Ray Joseph's SA Journos Who Tweet list.

This project is part of Media Monitoring Africa's AgendaSetters project, in partnership with School of Data.
